I am Stephen Hedman, one of the contributers for the project. My home is in Brooklyn Park, Minnesota, and I am a current student of the University of St Thomas. I am majoring in Applied Mathematics and will be graduating in Spring 2021. I did most of the work on getting the table and map set up.

Project details

The project used Nominatim for getting the geographic locations of places, and it used leaflet for the interactive map.

Demo

You can enter a location in the search field and clicking go will send the map to the location if it is within the St Paul bounds. You can click the markers to see the number of crimes committed in each neighborhood. When you move the map, the table will only show the crimes committed in the neighborhoods that are still visible on the map.
